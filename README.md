Music & Google Search 🎶
Music & Google Search is a powerful, integrated workspace that combines a professional music player with a dedicated Google search engine. Designed for productivity and seamless multitasking, it provides a sleek, dark-themed, and draggable interface.

✨ Key Features
Music Player: Load your audio files, manage your library, and enjoy synchronized lyrics via LRCLIB.

Google Search: A built-in Google search engine window, always ready for your queries.

Paste Functionality: Easily paste text from your clipboard directly into the Google Search bar with the dedicated Paste button.

Interactive Design: Draggable, resizable, and blur-effect windows for a professional studio feel.

Seamless Workflow: Switch between music control and web search without switching tabs or windows.

🛠 Usage
Load Tracks: Use the file input in the Music player window to load your songs.

Control Audio: Play, pause, navigate between tracks, or use the skip buttons to control your audio.

Search: Type your queries in the Google Search window or hit the Paste button to instantly search for copied text.

Lyrics: Synchronized lyrics will display automatically as your music plays.

🚀 Technical Specifications
Core: Vanilla JavaScript for high speed and stability.

Styling: Modern CSS3 with glassmorphism effects.

Lyrics API: Integrated with LRCLIB for real-time synchronization.
